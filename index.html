About Me

Turning Data Into Intelligence

I'm a final-year Computer Science student at Sai Gon University, Ho Chi Minh City. My passion lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and finance — building systems that can learn from market dynamics and make intelligent decisions autonomously.

My thesis on Group-Agent Reinforcement Learning (GARL) for stock trading strategies earned a score of 9.5/10, demonstrating deep expertise in multi-agent systems and quantitative finance. I also co-authored a Springer publication on explainable AI for fraud detection.

Beyond academics, I bring professional experience from my data analytics internship where I automated reporting workflows and analyzed healthcare datasets. I'm seeking opportunities to apply my ML and data engineering skills in industry.
